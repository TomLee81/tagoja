Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026 | Last Updated: May 1, 2026

TAGOJA, a brand operated by SEOWOO LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, including the Marketplace and Supply Engine services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, business details
  • Profile Information: Profile photos, business descriptions, verification documents
  • Transaction Information: Payment details, shipping addresses, order history
  • Communications: Messages, support inquiries, feedback, reviews

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (precise location only with consent)
  • Cookies and Tracking: See our Cookie Policy for details

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations, payment status
  • Logistics Partners: Shipping updates, delivery confirmations
  • Verification Services: Identity verification, business validation
  • Social Media: If you connect social accounts (with your permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Provide Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process transactions and payments
  • Facilitate buying, selling, and sourcing
  • Connect suppliers, sellers, and logistics partners
  • Provide customer support

2.2 Improve Platform

  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize features
  • Develop new products and services
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Test new features

2.3 Communicate

  • Send transaction confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support responses
  • Send marketing communications (with consent)
  • Notify about platform updates and changes

2.4 Security and Compliance

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect user safety and platform integrity

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

  • Buyers and Sellers: Name, ratings, reviews, transaction history (as needed for transactions)
  • Public Listings: Product details, seller information (as you choose to make public)

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Payment processors
  • Shipping and logistics partners
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Customer support tools
  • Marketing platforms (with consent)

3.3 Business Transfers

In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, your information may be transferred (you will be notified)

3.4 Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect rights and safety

3.5 With Your Consent

Any other sharing will be done with your explicit permission

4. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Active Accounts: As long as your account is active

  • Legal Hold: Longer if required by law or legal proceedings

You can request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements)

5.2 Marketing Communications

5.3 Account Management

  • Deactivate or delete your account
  • Download your data

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data

Important: However, no system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and protect your account credentials.
